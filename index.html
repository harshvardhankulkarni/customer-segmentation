Segment Breakdown

  • Champions (8.5%): Highest RFM scores. Drive 28% of revenue. Target with loyalty program.
  • Loyal Customers (38.5%): Regular buyers. Good upsell candidates.
  • Potential Loyalists (40%): Recent buyers. Need engagement campaigns.
  • At Risk (9%): High spend but not buying recently. Win-back campaigns needed.
  • Lost (4%): Low engagement. Clean from active marketing lists.

How It Works

Each customer gets scored 1-5 on three dimensions:

Recency
Days since last purchase. Lower is better.
Frequency
Total purchases. Higher is better.
Monetary
Total amount spent. Higher is better.

Combined RFM score (3-15) maps to one of five customer segments.
