Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

Demo Project

Segments 200 customers into 5 groups using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary scoring. Identifies high-value customers for retention and at-risk customers for re-engagement.

Output Chart

Key Results

  • Champions (8.5%): Highest RFM scores. Drive 28% of revenue.
  • Loyal Customers (38.5%): Regular buyers. Good targets for upsell.
  • Potential Loyalists (40%): Recent buyers. Need engagement campaigns.
  • At Risk (9%): High spend but not buying recently. Win them back.
  • Lost (4%): Low engagement. Clean from active lists.

Tech Stack

PythonPandasNumPyMatplotlibSeaborn

How It Works

Each customer gets scored 1-5 on three dimensions:

Recency
Days since last purchase
Lower is better
Frequency
Total number of purchases
Higher is better
Monetary
Total amount spent
Higher is better

Combined score (3-15) maps to one of five segments.
